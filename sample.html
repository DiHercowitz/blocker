Let's Block!

Look to this code.

<div class="blocker-example1">
  <div>
      <div class="box-content"> Lorem </div>
  </div>
  <div>
      <div class="box-content"> Ipsum </div>
  </div>
  <div>
      <div class="box-content"> Dolor </div>
  </div>
  <div>
      <div class="box-content"> Sit </div>
  </div>                    
</div>

The result is it:

Lorem
Ipsum
Dolor
Sit

Now, let's add the blocker class

<div class="blocker-4 blocker-example1">
  <div>
      <div class="box-content"> Lorem </div>
  </div>
  <div>
      <div class="box-content"> Ipsum </div>
  </div>
  <div>
      <div class="box-content"> Dolor </div>
  </div>
  <div>
      <div class="box-content"> Sit </div>
  </div>                    
</div>

Here we go!

Lorem
Ipsum
Dolor
Sit

Ok, the divs are to close! Don't worry, add the class blocker-grutter!

Lorem
Ipsum
Dolor
Sit

That is it! You can use blocker number 1 to 12 on wich element you want!
