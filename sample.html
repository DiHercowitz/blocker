Blocker

Forget about rework. This is a simple CSS solution to block contents side by side as columns.

Apply the Blocker class to the wrap element and all his childs will be displayed side by side and breakline if it is necesseary.

It is pure CSS. No Jquery.

I wish it could help someone :)

How to use?

You can choose witch elements is better on your interface. Basically, is the folow: 

<div class="blocker-[Number]">
  <div> something </div>
  <div> something </div>
  <div> something </div>
  <div> something </div>
</div>
